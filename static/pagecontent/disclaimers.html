Disclaimers

Living Document Disclaimer

Back in the olden times of LARP you would create some cool documents with your rules on them and adhere to them come what may - dying on multiple hills to preserve your precious, perfect vision. Those times are thankfully (mostly) behind us as a community.

That's why all our rules, background and game mechanics are subject to change if they don't survive contact with the reality of playing them out in an abandoned military base. These will be living documents and we will be hosting only the current version with changes to those documents will be communicated clearly through our social media presence.

We rely on honest and constructive feedback from our players to make our game better and we welcome post event feedback to our official email addresses which can be found elsewhere in this document.

Beta Rules and Feedback

To hammer the point above home - this version of the rules is the first Beta release (v0.1.0). We expect it as an inevitability that we'll soon be made aware of every flaw, every unintended interaction, and each ounce of inconsistency that we have missed.

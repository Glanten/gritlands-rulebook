Scout

Primarily concerned with assessing and surveying the blasted wasteland, Scouts tend to be tough, stealthy, survivors, able to assess the lay of the land, spot and circumvent the many hazards and dangers that the wastes, and the Hot Zones throw at them. They are INVALUABLE - without them you will get poisoned, irradiated, ambushed or worse.

Surveyor (NuGov)

Nu-Gov may despise the wasteland for its disorderly attempts to engulf the remnants of civilisations, but that doesn't mean they intend to remain ignorant about the dangers of the new world.

Scavenger (Wastelanders)

Of all the clans, the Wastelanders know best the dangers of the wasteland; and amongst the wastelanders, their scouts know best how to endure those dangers. The Wastelanders are nomadic, and they rely on their scavengers not only to identify reliable routes and find suitable shelter, but also to lead the clan to scrap and raw materials that they can trade for food, water, and medicine.

Quaestor (Knights of the Realm)

Quaestors eschew the beaten steel of the Knight so that they can more easily traverse the Roads and face the wastes in-between. They are tasked in keeping the scattered settlements of the Realm connected - no matter what. Outriders and scouts, the Quaestors are wise in identifying and neutralising the many dangers that lurk just off of the path.

Illuminator (The Unnumbered Host)

The Host has eyes and ears everywhere; some of them don't try to hide their role, and the Host's pilgrims act as watchers, informers, and forward observers. Whenever the Host finds a radioactive artefact they can enshrine, or a ruined structure they can re-purpose, or whenever they are forewarned of a razor-dust storm (so half of them can batten down the hatches while the other half strip naked and prepare to receive divine judgement from the wind), it is the pilgrims who identified what the wastes have in store...

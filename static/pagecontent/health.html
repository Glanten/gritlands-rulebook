Health

Combat and Hazards inflict HARM on a character.

If HARM isn't absorbed by the target character spending GRIT it inflicts a CONSEQUENCE on said character (CONSEQUENCES have both a role play and a mechanical effect).

Too many untreated consequences (4 as standard) and you DIE.

GRIEVOUS HARM is a form of HARM of such severity and intensity that a baseline human cannot hope to shrug it off - things like taking a bullet to the face, or a chainsaw to the gut. GRIEVOUS HARM cannot be absorbed by spending GRIT and automatically inflicts a CONSEQUENCE on its victim.

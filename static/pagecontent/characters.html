Characters

Players create Characters. Each Player can have only 1 character at any time - upon death, the newly-dead character no longer counts towards this limit. Characters are built by choosing a Clan and an Archetype - which defines both the pool of skills they can learn and how relatively difficult those skills are to develop.

Skills can:
  • Provide mechanical abilities otherwise unavailable
  • Allow the production of items (with use of resources or other limitations)
  • Allow the use of specialist equipment
  • Provide a resistance or protection
  • Allow the removal of obstacles
  • Grant some combination of the above

Characters start with 6 GRIT as standard. GRIT is a pool of points which are used to absorb HARM and to fuel abilities

Characters track HARM that they do not absorb with GRIT on their CONSEQUENCE TRACK

Characters start with 4 CONSEQUENCE SLOTS as standard

Characters have access to some EQUIPMENT based on their Clan/Skills or other factors

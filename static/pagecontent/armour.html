Armour

Armour grants additional GRIT - to benefit from this additional durability, a character must have a talent that allows them to use it.

War Paint

War Paint is any collection of trinkets, leathers, scrap or heraldry-emblazoned junk that matches a character's clan and archetype: it's the difference that makes a difference between life or death. Examples include: a NuGov enforcer's brass-buttoned 'interrogation jacket', a Wastelander blackthumb's lucky necklace of old spanners and grenade pins or a Knight of the Realm's robes of cultivation. All characters can wear War Paint.

As per the ...& Chewing Gum "War Paint" Talent, wearing War Paint provides your character with an additional 2 points of GRIT.

'Eavy Metal

'Eavy Metal on the other hand is unabashedly clank: it's there to stop an axe, turn aside claws and dissuade the cowardly from your path.

Only characters who invest in the 'Eavy Metal Talent (from the D-Fens Skill) can gain use of this level of protection.

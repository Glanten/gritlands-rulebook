Mutations

Mutations are a special set of Talents under the ...& Chewing Gum Skill.

In addition to the skill point cost for the mutation each one requires a high quality prosthetic and/or costume modification that is appropriate to represent the Mutation/ability chosen.

Mutant Prosthetics and Makeup

Choosing to be a mutant is a serious commitment in terms of kit, costume and makeup. Ear tips and a bit of snazz will not cut it. Ending the day with half your makeup rubbed off on your sleeve and two spirit gum circles where your horns used to be is no good either.

Prosthetics should be high quality, durable. Make up should be nuanced and tough enough to withstand an athletic day in the zone. Everything should be perky and seem alive - no flaccid tails or tentacles hanging limply from your waist... Body parts should look connected - not like a pair of gloves/shoes/shoulder pads with spikes glued to them.

The hassles are a poor trade off for the abilities you get access to. Do it because you love the concept.

First Mutation

All Mutants must take the following Talent from their ...& Chewing Gum Skill:

  • The First (Mutagen) is Free... (0 skill points)

The player must display a clear cosmetic mutation. This should be obvious and unmistakable. It could consist of several of the following:

  • Skin marking / patterns across the face, neck, forearms and hands
  • Horns
  • Teeth / fangs / tusks
  • Facial prosthetics
  • Unnatural eyes
  • Spines
  • Growths / Goiters / things on or protruding from the body
  • Webbed fingers
  • Stunted wings
  • Fur patches

Further Mutations

Enhanced Senses

1 skill point; can be taken up to 3 times

Your senses are enhanced. Each time you take this skill you gain the ability to read Scout Level 1 TAGs once per day (which you may swap for Ambush knowledge before you enter the zone). If your character already has the Scout skill then they instead receive an additional random resource per level in this mutation as per the Scavenger Talent that they are able to snaffle out.

Prosthetic - each time you take this mutation:

  • Oversized/exaggerated/misshapen sense organs (ears, eyes, nose, tongue?, hands)
  • New/additional sense organs (additional eyes, nostrils, antenna)
Combat Adaptation

1 skill point; can be taken up to 3 times

You may take one of the 'weapon call' Talents from the Carnage Skill (MAIM, Strikedown or Disarm), and can use it twice per day. It costs GRIT to use. (if you already have the skill you get 1 one additional free use per day)

Prosthetic - each time you take this mutation:

  • Bulging muscles (for Strikedown)
  • Tentacles (for anything)
  • Entangling spines (for Disarm)
  • Ravening fangs, barbs and spikes (for MAIM)
Venom Sacs

1 skill point

Up to a number of times per day equal to your ...& Chewing Gum level you can secrete a debilitating poison By spending 10 seconds roleplaying the application of this poison to a weapon and expendign 1 GRIT, you ma make a single "MAIM" Call in the next 1 minute.

Prosthetics:

  • Brightly coloured pulsating sacs on your neck / face / throat
Gills

1 skill point

For 1 point of GRIT you can breathe as if you are wearing a functional gas mask for 5 minutes.

Prosthetics:

  • Gill slits or exposed sea-slug style gill frills
Armour Plates

1 skill point

You gain 1 additional point of GRIT that can only be used for absorbing (non-grievous) harm.

Prosthetics:

  • Thick armoured plates - chitin or bone obvious on exposed skin, and increased bulk under your costume / armour
Conductive Skin

1 skill point

Update: You are resistant to electrical sources of HARM (including Tasers). You can expend 5 2 batteries to generate a shockingly powerful electric attack by touch up to 3 times by calling 'HARM' to deal 1 HARM. Alternatively, if using a suitably conductive appearing weapon physrep you may call 'STRIKEDOWN' instead.

Battery is an engineer recipe.

Prosthetics:

  • Metallic skin
  • Metallic markings on your skin
  • Prominent, current-generating organs
  • Metallic hair
  • Metallic filaments extruding from your body
Photosynthesis

1 skill point

Regain 1 GRIT every 4 hours during the day (7am until 7pm). Lose 2 GRIT when the Sun goes down (after 7pm) and reduce your maximum GRIT by 2 until 7am the next day.

Prosthetics:

  • Variegated Greenish skin and plant-like fronds
'Tenz Gun

1 skill point

You can fire a bone-like dart from a bizarre organ using a powerful muscular spasm, chemical reaction or freakish act of biomechanics. Once fired, you must wait one minute before using this mutation again - or spend 1 GRIT to use it immediately.

Prosthetics:

  • A Nerf gun capable of firing a single MEGA dart hidden inside some strange organic-seeming protrusion
  • You are allowed to reclaim this specially-marked, distinct dart for reuse
  • If you lose the dart a new one will grow slowly and be issued by GOD within a few hours
Monstrous Metabolism

1 skill point

Once per Day, whilst in the zone - can spend 5 minutes consuming biomatter (resource or corpses) to regain GRIT equal to your ...& Chewing Gum level.

Prosthetics:

  • Horrifying fangs
  • Discoloured, distended skin
  • Expansive paunch or gaping maw
Unnatural Strength

1 skill point; can be taken up to 3 times

You may count your Unnatural Strength level against GRIT expenditure for certain TASKS such as breaking things or carrying things as per the details on the TASK description.

Prosthetics:

  • Immense bulk and musculature
Regeneration

3 skill points

Once per day you can stabilise a Major or Critical consequence you have suffered.

Once per day you may immediately recover from a moderate or Major consequence.

Prosthetics:

  • Regeneration nodes with thick, weird veins across all exposed skin
  • Distended, rubbery flesh with horribly scarified patches of regenerated meat

Illuminator

Unnumbered Host

The Host has eyes and ears everywhere; some of them don't try to hide their role, and the Host's pilgrims act as watchers, informers, and forward observers. Whenever the Host finds a radioactive artefact they can enshrine, or a ruined structure they can re-purpose, or whenever they are forewarned of a razor-dust storm (so half of them can batten down the hatches while the other half strip naked and prepare to receive divine judgement from the wind), it is the pilgrims who identified what the wastes have in store...

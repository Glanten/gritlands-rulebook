Bonesaw: Some mistake the Wastelander's wasteland survival viewpoint to mean that they regard the wounded as “weak” and leave them to bleed out and die. This is not the case. The Wastelanders are like a single organism, and like any organism it can be damaged and weakened by its trials in the wastes; the difference is that the Wastelanders know best how to heal that organism and bring it back to full strength, this time criss-crossed with hella sweet scar tissue and the determination to keep going...

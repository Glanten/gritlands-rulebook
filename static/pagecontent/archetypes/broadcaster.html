Broadcaster: If no-one saw you do it, did it even happen? The Story Keepers of the Wastelanders are there to safeguard the stories of its heroes. As well as keeping these great tales of daring-do alive they also serve as a vast repository of knowledge; which yellow snow not to eat, which wasteland fauna make great pillows, how many cactuses Big Jim was able to cram in his mouth in one go. They are often identified by the tools of their trade, microphones and empty screened frames with which to broadcast their knowledge to all those willing to listen.

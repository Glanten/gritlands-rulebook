GRIT

Every Character starts with a pool of 6 GRIT. This can be modified by Skills, Talents and Equipment.

GRIT is a pool of points that can be used for a variety of purposes:

What GRIT can do:
  • Avoid a CONSEQUENCE from taking HARM (unless the damage is GRIEVOUS, see below)
  • Avoid a CONSEQUENCE from environmental damage
  • Fuel special abilities or the use of some skills
  • Perform certain actions

What GRIT cannot do:
  • Avoid a consequence from GRIEVOUS HARM (such as that inflicted by a gunshot)
  • Restore or remove a consequence you took previously
  • Regenerate quickly on its own

GRIT Details

GRIT is measure of your characters mental and physical fortitude, luck, determination, karma and creativity.

GRIT has many, many uses in the game:

  • Avoiding Harm - anytime you take HARM (NOT when you take GRIEVOUS HARM) you may spend one grit from your pool to ignore that harm and avoid taking a consequence.
  • Powering Skills - many skills require you to spend 1 (or more) points of Grit in order to activate their more powerful effects. Details are found in individual skill descriptions.
  • Performing TASKS - most tasks require some element of GRIT expenditure.
  • Overcoming Obstacles - many obstacles in the Hotzone can be overcome through sheer determination, tenacity, luck or creativity. The INFO TAG on the Obstacle will provide the details you need to understand the options available to you.
  • Other stuff - GRIT has other uses as well, which will be made clear as and when you can use it.

Equipment, Consumables and GRIT

There are items available in the game that can be used in place of spending a point of GRIT or add to the GRIT being spent.

Some of these may only be used in certain circumstances or for certain purposes. These uses will be clear in the description of the item (see Equipment for more details).

Recovering GRIT

GRIT is fully restored every morning at time-in.

During the course of the day players may find opportunities to restore 1 or more points of GRIT through the use of skills, equipment or consumables - details can be found in the individual descriptions.

Traditions: each Clan has specific traditions that they can use to regain GRIT. Leading the ritual requires a specific skill, but all members of the Clan can benefit from it (if they participate) without needing any special skills.

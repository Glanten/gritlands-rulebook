Resistance

Everypony

You are better adapted to the rigours of the Gritlands than most.

Level 1
  • Twice per day you can avoid HARM inflicted by the environment without spending GRIT.
Level 2
  • Thrice per day you can avoid HARM inflicted by the environment without spending GRIT.
Level 3
  • Quadrice (is that even a word?) per day you can avoid HARM inflicted by the environment without spending GRIT.

Resistance Talents

Iodine of the Soul (1 skill point)

May be taken once per level of Resistance.

An additional once per day you can resit HARM from a RADIATION source without spending GRIT.

Difficult to Defrag (1 skill point)

May be taken once per level of Resistance.

An additional once per day you may resist HARM from a NANITE source without spending GRIT.

Lung Capacity (1 skill point)

May be taken once per level of Resistance.

An additional once per day you may avoid HARM from a GAS source without spending GRIT.

Resistance

Everypony

You are better adapted to the rigours of the Gritlands than most.

This is the skill to take if you want to brush off environmental hazards in the HOTZONE; it is an invaluable skill for Scouts and anyone who thinks they might be in the HOTZONE for longer than they had planned.

For example, if the HOTZONE inflicted 1 point of harm every 5 minutes, you might use this skill to soak that damage (without using GRIT, see below) for a few cycles, depending on your skill level, before having to take Consequences from staying in the HOTZONE for too long. Alternatively, you might use this skill to safely pass through a field of electrical discharge that would otherwise cause you a severe injury.

Level 1
  • Twice per day, you may avoid harm from an environmental hazard without spending GRIT.
Level 2
  • As level 1 plus:
  • Gain an additional free use of this skill to avoid environmental harm.
Level 3
  • As level 2 plus:
  • Gain yet another free use of this skill to avoid environmental harm.

Resistance Talents

Iodine of the Soul (1 skill point)

May be taken once per level of Resistance.

An additional once per day you can resist HARM from a RADIATION source without spending GRIT.

Difficult to Defrag (1 skill point)

May be taken once per level of Resistance.

An additional once per day you may resist HARM from a NANITE source without spending GRIT.

Lung Capacity (1 skill point)

May be taken once per level of Resistance.

An additional once per day you may avoid HARM from a GAS source without spending GRIT.

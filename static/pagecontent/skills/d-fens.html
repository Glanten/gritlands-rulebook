Design Notes

Resistance to calls

We almost didn't include these abilities, because having your jam trump my jam isn't much fun. Gritlands isn't intended to be a PVP game, if you get in a scrap with another player - this skill means you might need to think about when you deploy your weapon calls, but cannot counter them entirely (as you do not spend the grit if the effect is not taken).

What we absolutely will not do is routinely give resistance skills to the general opposition. We might give a limited subset of the opposition limited resistances where it makes narrative sense (if it's using its claws and horns to gouge you, you can't disarm it. If it is a 2 ton Automated defence turret you can't strike it down.)

Coordinator

Everypony

You are good at organising disparate teams towards a common goal.

If there are multiple TASKS occuring at the same time and you can roleplay appropriately with all of the participants involved, you may spend 1 GRIT to add 1 GRIT to each of the TASKS being performed, even if you wouldn't normally be able to assist with the TASK at hand. This can be done a limited number of times per day (see below).

Essentially, Coordinator allows you to assist with multiple TASKS but only pay the GRIT cost of assisting with a single TASK.

Level 1

You can use Coordinator once per day.

Level 2

You can use Coordinator twice per day.

Level 3

You can use Coordinator thrice per day.

Coordinator Talents

Hospital Administrator (1 Skill point)

If you use Coordinator in a hospital zone you control, you grant an additional 1 GRIT to every Counselling, First Aid, Medic, or Psychoanalysis TASK being performed in the hospital zone. This talent has a 20 minute cooldown between uses.

Note that a hospital Zone's support is limited to a maximum of 4 patients.

Royal Heir II (1 skill point)

Requires Level 3 Coordinator.

Once per day you may spend 2 GRIT in order to add 2 GRIT to each TASK you are coordinating.

Strategist (1 skill point)

Requires Level 3 Coordinator.

You have an additional daily use of the Coordinator skill.

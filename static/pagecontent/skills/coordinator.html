Coordinator

Everypony

You are good at organising disparate teams towards a common goal.

Level 1
  • Once per day if there are multiple TASKS occurring at the same time and you can role play appropriately with each of the groups involved you can spend 1 GRIT to add 1 GRIT to the total for each of the TASKS involved.
Level 2
  • Twice per day if there are multiple TASKS occurring at the same time and you can role play appropriately with each of the groups involved you can spend 1 GRIT to add 1 GRIT to the total for each of the TASKS involved.
Level 3
  • Thrice per day if there are multiple TASKS occurring at the same time and you can role play appropriately with each of the groups involved you can spend 1 GRIT to add 1 GRIT to the total for each of the TASKS involved.

Coordinator Talents

Hospital Administrator (1 Skill point)

Every 20 minutes you can spend 1 GRIT to provide 1 additional grit to every medic or nurse working in the hospital zone you control. A hospital Zone can support up to 4 patients.

Royal Heir II (1 skill point)

Requires Level 3 Coordinator.

Once per day you may spend 2 GRIT to add 2 to each TASK you are coordinating.

Strategist (1 skill point)

Requires Level 3 Coordinator.

You have one additional use of the coordination skill per day.

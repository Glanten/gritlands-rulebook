Thicker than Water

Everypony

In the wasteland your first, last and best survival tool is your group, or possibly a G2000 Slaughter Bot. But who can find one of those in good condition with less than 1,500 kills on the clock?

Thicker Than Water lets you redistribute a limited amount of GRIT between members of your Group. This might give an otherwise-exhausted Engineer just enough pep to perform a crucial TASK, give a Warrior the strength to open a sealed chamber, or empower scientist to save a comrade's life despite extreme pressure.

Level 1

You may lead a ritual involving 2 minutes of appropriate roleplaying with members of your Group. All participants may donate up to 1 GRIT to a central pool; this pool can then be granted to one recipient in your Group.

All characters involved in this rite must be present, must have the Thicker Than Water skill, and must agree to the redistribution.

Level 2

As Level 1 except each participant may donate up to 2 GRIT to the pool.

Level 3

As Level 1 except each participant may donate up to 3 GRIT to the pool.

Thicker than Water Talents

Close as Siblings (1 skill point)

The GRIT pool may be shared between 2 recipients instead of 1. The distribution need not be even.

Synergy (1 skill point)

Once per day when participating in Thicker Than Water, you may add 1 GRIT to the pool for free.

Well Oiled Machine (1 skill point)

Once per day when participating in Thicker Than Water, you may add 1 GRIT per participant to the pool for free.

Thicker than Water

Everypony

In the wasteland your first, last and best survival tool is your group, or possibly a G2000 Slaughter Bot. But who can find one of those in good condition with less than 1,500 kills on the clock?

Level 1
  • By performing 2 minutes of appropriate role-playing, you may take up to 1 GRIT from each player in your group and redistribute it to another player in your group. All players involved must be present, have the Thicker than Water skill, and agree to the redistribution.
Level 2
  • By performing 2 minutes of appropriate role-playing, you may take up to 2 GRIT from each player in your group and redistribute it to another player in your group. All players involved must be present, have the Thicker than Water skill, and agree to the redistribution.
Level 3
  • By performing 2 minutes of appropriate role-playing, you may take up to 2 GRIT from each player in your group and redistribute it to another player in your group. All players involved must be present, have the Thicker than Water skill, and agree to the redistribution.

Thicker than Water Talents

Close as Siblings (1 skill point)

The donated pool of points may be shared between 2 recipients.

Synergy (1 skill point)

Once per day - Add 1 GRIT per level of Thicker than water to the donated pool before redistributing.

Well Oiled Machine (1 skill point)

Once per day add 1 GRIT per donating member to the donated pool before redistributing.

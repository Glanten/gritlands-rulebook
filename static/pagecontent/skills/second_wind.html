Second Wind

Everypony

The Gritlands will wear you down, chew you up and spit you out. The ability to soldier on, come what may, in the face of adversity ad infinitum... that separates the ADULTS from the ChilDREN. HanG iN TheRe.

This skill can be used once per day, requires appropriate roleplaying, and has the following restrictions:

  • Requires 30 seconds of roleplay.
  • You cannot take any combat actions during this time.
  • You must remain within approximately 2 metres of the point where you start using the skill.
  • If you receive any harm during this period you must restart the 30 seconds of roleplaying.

Second Wind is a restorative skill that lets you reclaim your GRIT and keep going when others might fall behind.

Level 1

When using Second Wind, you regain 2 GRIT.

Level 2

When using Second Wind, you regain 3 GRIT.

Level 3

When using Second Wind, you regain 4 GRIT.

Second Wind Talents

Gale Force (3 skill points)

When you regain GRIT with Second Wind, regain 1 additional point of GRIT.

Tornado (3 skill points)

When using Second Wind, you may restore 1 GRIT to one nearby character for every level of Second Wind you have. Your roleplaying should including challenging or encouraging your allies.

Characters you restore with this talent must be within 2 meters of you and must not be engaged in combat.

Maelstrom (3 skill points)

Requires Second Wind 3.

When you consume your last use of Second Wind, you gain one use of Maelstrom.

Maelstrom has the same restrictions and requirements as Second Wind and gives you 2 GRIT.

Your use of Maelstrom expires at the end of the day.

Easy Breezy (1 point)

If you spend 10 minutes roleplaying instead of 30 seconds, you regain an additional 1 GRIT.

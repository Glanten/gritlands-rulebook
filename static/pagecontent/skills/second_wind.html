This skill can be used once per day, requires appropriate roleplaying, and has the following restrictions:

  • Requires 30 seconds of roleplay.
  • You cannot take any combat actions during this time.
  • You must remain within approximately 2 metres of the point where you start using the skill.
  • If you receive any HARM during this period you must restart the 30 seconds.

Level 1
  • You regain 2 GRIT.
Level 2
  • You regain 3 GRIT.
Level 3
  • You regain 4 GRIT.

Second Wind Talents

Gale Force (3 skill points)

When you regain GRIT with Second wind you regain 1 additional point.

Tornado (3 skill points)

When you use your second wind ability you may allow up to (second wind skill level) other characters within 6 feet to regain1 GRIT each. Your 30 seconds of roleplay should include encouraging them. They cannot be included if they are in combat.

Maelstrom (3 skill points)

Requires Second Wind level 3

If you have used your Second wind ability - you may use Maelstrom once per day to regain 2 GRIT.

Easy Breezy (1 point)

If you spend 10 minutes instead of 30 seconds you regain an additional 1 point of GRIT.

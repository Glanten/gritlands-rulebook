Medic

Archetype, Professional

You can patch up and repair all manner of injuries, often for good.

Focus: A medical kit, and a field hospital setting.

Level 1
  • Once Per Day you can Heal a minor consequence. 2 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional minor consequences for 1 GRIT each.
  • For 2 points of GRIT you can Heal a Moderate consequence. 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can HEAL a Stabilised Major Consequence. 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a Major Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to gain a CRITICAL consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a CRITICAL Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to TERMINAL. There is nothing more you can do for them. Smh.
  • You cannot use this skill to heal your own Consequences.
  • You can read level 1 Medic and Forensic TAGs and undertake Medic and Forensic TASKS.
Level 2
  • Twice Per Day you can Heal a minor consequence. 2 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional minor consequences for 1 GRIT each.
  • Once per day you can Heal a Moderate consequence. 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional Moderate consequences for 2 GRIT each.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can HEAL a Stabilised Major Consequence. 5 minutes of roles play. The consequence is removed.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a Major Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to gain a CRITICAL consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a CRITICAL Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to TERMINAL. There is nothing more you can do for them. Smh.
  • You cannot use this skill to heal your own Consequences.
  • You can read level 1 and 2 Medic and Forensic TAGs and undertake Medic and Forensic TASKS.
Level 3
  • Thrice Per Day you can Heal a minor consequence. 2 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional minor consequences for 1 GRIT.
  • Twice per day you can Heal a Moderate consequence. 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional Moderate consequences for 2 GRIT each.
  • Once per Day you can HEAL a Stabilised Major Consequence -OR- Stabilise a Major -OR- Critical Consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can HEAL a Stabilised Major Consequence. 5 minutes of roles play. The consequence is removed.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a Major Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to gain a CRITICAL consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a CRITICAL Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to TERMINAL. There is nothing more you can do for them. Smh.
  • You cannot use this skill to heal your own Consequences.
  • You can read level 1, 2 and 3 Medicine and Forensic TAGs and undertake Medic and Forensic TASKS.

Medic Talents

Physician Heal thyself (1 skill point)

You can heal your own Minor Consequences for 2 GRIT.

Field Medic (1 skill point)

For 1 additional point of GRIT you can HEAL Minor, Moderate Consequences or Stabilise Major or Critical Consequences without a Field Hospital Focus. (note the bead pull will be disadvantaged).

If you have the Surgeon Talent you can attempt Surgery without a field Hospital. (the bead pull will be disadvantaged x3)

Surgeon (1 skill point)

(Medic Level) times per day you can perform surgery on a character suffering from a stabilised. CRITICAL Consequence.

Surgery can HEAL a Critical Consequence.

Surgery requires a Field Hospital.

Surgery TASKs require a Bead Pull.

Steady Hands (1 skill point)

This talent requires the Surgeon Talent

When performing Surgery you get an advantage on the bead pull.

Intuitive Medicine (1 skill point)

You gain advantage on bead pulls for Medicine and Surgery.

Trauma Medic (1 skill point)

If you are healing consequences caused by Physical sources (eg: combat) you spend 1 GRIT less.

Systemic Pathologist (1 skill point)

If you are healing consequences caused by Environmental sources you spend 1 less GRIT.

Forensic Pathologist (1 skill point)

You gain advantage on Bead pulls when carrying out forensic TASKS.

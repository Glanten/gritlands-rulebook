Medic

Archetype, Professional

You can patch up and repair all manner of injuries, often for good.

Skill Focus: A (relatively) clean and well-stocked medical kit; a field hospital setting.

A field hospital can hold four patients at a time. Once a field hospital is at capacity, you'd better hope there are more facilities nearby or that someone can provide First Aid to stymie the flow of additional patients.

Medic is the go-to skill to remove physical Consequences (wounds, bleeding, burns, broken bones, etc.) from a character, especially the really bad kind of Consequences that threaten their lives. It's restrictive, however, in that it requires a field hospital; so if you need a quick fix in a HOTZONE you might want to look to First Aid instead. Be aware also that Medic does nothing for Consequences to the psyche, for those you want the Counselling and Psychoanalysis skills.

Level 1
  • You may spend 1 GRIT and engage in 2 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to treat a Minor (Physical) Consequence. This treatment removes the Consequence entirely from the patient. There is no GRIT cost for this skill the first time you use it on a given day.
  • You may spend 2 GRIT and engage in 5 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to treat a Moderate (Physical) Consequence. This treatment removes the Consequence entirely from the patient.
  • You may spend 3 GRIT and engage in 5 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to treat a Major (Physical) Consequence, so long as it has been stabilised. This treatment removes the Consequence entirely from the patient.
  • You may spend 3 GRIT and attempt to stabilise a Major (Physical) Consequence with a series of bead-pulls. 3 successful bead-pulls stabilises the Consequence, whereas 3 unsuccessful bead-pulls worsens the condition to a Critical Consequence. You must engage in 1 minute of appropriate roleplaying between each bead-pull.
  • You may spend 3 GRIT and attempt to stabilise a Critical (Physical) Consequence with a series of bead-pulls. 3 successful bead-pulls stabilises the Consequence, whereas 3 unsuccessful bead-pulls will inflict a Terminal condition on the character (at this point their fate is sealed and there is nothing more you can do for them). You must engage in 1 minute of appropriate roleplaying between each bead-pull.
  • You may not use this skill to treat your own Consequences.
Level 2
  • As level 1 plus:
  • You gain an additional free daily use of this skill to treat Minor (Physical) Consequences.
  • You gain a free daily use of this skill to treat a Moderate (Physical) Consequence.
  • You still may not use this skill to treat your own Consequences.
Level 3
  • As level 2 plus:
  • You gain another free daily use of this skill to treat Minor (Physical) Consequences.
  • You gain a second free daily use of this skill to treat a Moderate (Physical) Consequence.
  • Once per day you may perform one of the following actions with the normal time and roleplaying requirements, but with no GRIT cost or bead-pull:
    • Treat a stabilised Major (Physical) Consequence
    • Stabilise a Major (Physical) Consequence
    • Stabilise a Critical (Physical) Consequence
  • Thou shalt not use this skill to heal your own Consequences.

Medic Talents

Physician Heal thyself (1 skill point)

You may treat your own Minor (Physical) Consequences in the same way as you would treat another character, but the base cost of treating your own Consequence is 2 GRIT and you may not use a free daily use of this skill to treat yourself.

Field Medic (1 skill point)

You can spend 1 point of GRIT per treatment to eschew the need for a field hospital as a Skill Focus.

Even with this talent, bead-pulls without a field hospital will be at disadvantage (draw twice and keep the worse result).

If you have the Surgeon talent (see below), you may also attempt surgery without a field hospital, but your bead-pulls will be at severe disadvantage (draw three times and keep the worse result).

Surgeon (1 skill point)

(Medic Level) times per day you can perform surgery on a character suffering from a stabilised CRITICAL Consequence.

Surgery can HEAL a Critical Consequence.

Surgery TASKs require a Bead Pull.

Steady Hands (1 skill point)

Requires the Surgeon talent.

Bead-pulls for the Surgery talent are drawn at advantage (draw twice and take the better result).

Intuitive Medicine (1 skill point)

Bead-pulls for the Medic Tasks and Surgery Tasks are drawn at advantage (draw twice and take the better result).

Trauma Medic (1 skill point)

The GRIT cost to treat a (Physical) Consequence that was caused by combat (gunshots, arrows, knives, teeth claws, etc.) is reduced by 1.

Systemic Pathologist (1 skill point)

The GRIT cost to treat a (Physical) Consequence that was caused by an environmental hazard (fire, radiation, toxic gas, etc.) is reduced by 1.

Forensic Pathologist (1 skill point)

When attempting a Forensic TASK, your draw bead-pulls at advantage (draw twice and take better result).

Teamwork

Everypony

Teamwork makes the Pixar work. Many hands make light Broth. A problem shared is annoying. Birds of a feather are killed with one stone.

Level 1
  • If you are participating (spending GRIT) in a TASK to with 3 or more participants and all the participants are from your Group:
  • Count your GRIT Contribution as +1 point more than you spend (your minimum spend must be 1 Grit).
Level 2
  • If you are participating (spending GRIT) in a TASK to with 3 or more participants and all the participants are from your Group:
  • Count your GRIT Contribution as +2 points more than you spend (your minimum spend must be 1 Grit).
Level 3
  • If you are participating (spending GRIT) in a TASK to with 3 or more participants and all the participants are from your Group:
  • Count your GRIT Contribution as +3 point more than you spend (your minimum spend must be 1 Grit).

Teamwork Talents

Easy going (1 skill point)

Once per day you can use Team work without spending GRIT to add your level in Teamwork to the GRIT Pool.

A Broad Church (1 skill point)

Your Teamwork skill still can be used if the number of participants that are not from your group (but still from your clan) is equal to or less than your Teamwork skill level. All remaining participants must be from your group.

A Very Broad Church (3 skill points)

Requires A Broad Church.

Your Teamwork skill still can be used if the number of participants that are not from your group is equal to or less than your Teamwork skill level. All remaining participants must be from your group.

This Talent cannot be used at the same time as A Broad Church.

Note: when using A broad Church and A very Broad Church each individual with the skill must fulfil the requirements for themselves - they are not in any way cumulative.

True Grit

Everypony

Even by the standards of most survivors in the Gritlands - you are especially tough, wily or lucky.

Level 1
  • You have an additional point of GRIT.
Level 2
  • You have two additional points of GRIT.
Level 3
  • You have three additional points of GRIT.

True Grit Talents

Lucky (1 Skill Point)

Once per day you can add advantage to a bead pull, if you are spending your own GRIT on the TASK

Tough (1 skill Point)

Once per day you can soak a point of (Physical, non-GRIEVOUS) HARM without spending GRIT.

Wily (1 skill Point)

Once per day you can count your GRIT total for a TASK you are undertaking as 1 point higher than the GRIT you spend.

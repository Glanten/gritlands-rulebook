True Grit

Everypony

Even by the standards of most survivors in the Gritlands - you are especially tough, wily or lucky.

Level 1

You have 1 additional point of GRIT.

Level 2

As level 1 plus:

You have another 1 additional point of GRIT.

Level 3

As level 2 plus:

You have yet another 1 additional point of GRIT!

>

True Grit Talents

Lucky (1 Skill Point)

Once per day, if you are spending GRIT on a TASK that involves a bead-pull, you may draw at advantage (draw twice and keep the better result).

Tough (1 skill Point)

Once per day, you may ignore one point of Physical, non-grievous harm without spending GRIT to do so.

Wily (1 skill Point)

Once per day, if you are spending GRIT on a TASK, you may add 1 GRIT to the TASK for free.

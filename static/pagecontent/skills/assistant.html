Assistant

Everypony

In order to survive the Gritlands you have to support and be supported by your friends, family, clan and even the coalition. And you're really good at helping others achieve their full potential.

The Assistant skill allows you to spend GRIT on a TASK on behalf of another character, this means you can assist with a TASK that would otherwise be limited to a lone participant.

If the skill you are assisting is a Professional skill (e.g. Pharmacology or the Engineering skill), you can only assist if you have points in that skill (or a talent that counts as that skill) and can only contribute GRIT up to your level in that skill.

Level 1
  • You may contribute 1 GRIT to a TASK by assisting another character.
Level 2
  • You may contribute up to 2 GRIT to a TASK by assisting another character.
Level 3
  • You may contribute 3 GRIT to a TASK by assisting another character.

Assistant Talents

Best Boy (1 Skill point)

You can assist with Engineering TASKS as if you had the Engineer skill at level 1.

Pink Man (1 Skill point)

You can assist with Science TASKS as if you had the Pharmacology skill at level 1.

Surgical Nurse (1 Skill point)

You can assist with Medicine TASKS as if you had the Medic skill at level 1.

Triage Nurse (1 Skill point)

You can assist with First Aid TASKS as if you had the First Aid skill at level 1.

Mortuary Ghoul (1 Skill point)

You can assist with Forensics TASKS as if you had the Medic skill at level 1.

Semi-Pro (1 Skill point)

Requires Assistant level 2.

If you can assist with a Professional skill (by having the skill at level 1 or higher, or via a talent), you may treat your effective skill level as 2 instead of its true value.

Professional (1 Skill point)

Requires Assistant level 3 and the Semi-Pro talent.

If you can assist with a Professional skill (by having the skill at level 1 or higher, or via a talent), you may treat your effective skill level as 3 instead of its true value.

Assistant

Everypony

In order to survive the Gritlands you have to support and be supported by your friends, family, clan and even the coalition. And you're really good at helping others achieve their full potential.

If the skill being used is a professional skill you can only assist if you have the same skill (or a talent that counts as that skill) and can only contribute GRIT up to your level in that skill.

Level 1
  • Once per TASK you can spend up to 1 point of GRIT towards the completion of the TASK on behalf of another player, provided only you and they are working on the TASK.
Level 2
  • Once per TASK you can spend up to 2 points of GRIT towards the completion of the TASK on behalf of another player, provided only you and they are working on the TASK.
Level 3
  • Once per TASK you can spend up to 3 points of GRIT towards the completion of the TASK on behalf of another player, provided only you and they are working on the TASK.

Note: Because Assisstant allows you to spend GRIT on behalf of another character you can assist with a task that has a maximum of 1 participant.

Assistant Talents

Best Boy (1 Skill point)

You count as having the engineer skill at level 1 if assisting with an engineering TASK.

Pink man (1 Skill point)

You count as having the pharmacology skill at level 1 if assisting a science TASK.

Surgical Nurse (1 Skill point)

You count as having the medic skill at level 1 if assisting with a medicine TASK.

Triage Nurse (1 Skill point)

You count as having the first aid skill at level 1 if assisting with a first aid TASK.

Mortuary Ghoul (1 Skill point)

You count as having the medic skill at level 1 if assisting with forensics TASKS.

Semi - pro (1 Skill point)

Requires Assistant at level 2

When assisting with a professional skill you may count your effective skill level as 2 (whether you have the skill at 1, or have a talent that counts as that skill at level 1).

Professional (1 Skill point)

Requires semi-pro, and requires assistant at level 3.

When assisting with a professional skill you may count your effective skill level as 3 (whether you have the skill at 1, or have a talent that counts as that skill at level 1).

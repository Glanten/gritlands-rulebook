Pharmacology

Archetype, Professional

You know how to brew, cook, concoct, distil and react various chemicals together.

Skill Focus: A chemistry work bench complete with test tubes, beakers, coloured liquids etc.

Pharmacology is a crafting skill that lets you create consumable items by combining raw materials (normally found in HOTZONES) according to the recipes your character knows. Pharmacology also covers scientific knowledge and methods, allowing you to interact with certain elements in the game that would otherwise remain a mystery.

Level 1
  • You know 3 Chemistry recipes.
Level 2
  • As level 1 plus:
  • You know another 3 Chemistry recipes.
Level 3
  • You know yet another 4 Chemistry recipes.

Pharmacology Talents

Additional Recipes (1 skill point)

You know 2 additional recipes each time you take this skill.

This talent may be taken once for level of Pharmacology you have.

Monarch Reagent (1 skill point)

You make Science TASK bead pulls at advantage (pull twice and take the better result).

Heisenberg (1 skill point)

Your concoctions are more potent; Pharmacology items you craft from recipes you know last 1 minute longer for every level you have in Pharmacology.

Pharmaceutical Engineer (1 skill point)

The effectiveness of any medicines you brew are increased.

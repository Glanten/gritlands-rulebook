Pharmacology

Archetype, Professional

You know how to Brew, cook, concoct, distil and react various chemicals together.

Focus: Chemistry work bench - test tubes, beakers, coloured liquids etc.

Level 1
  • You know 3 Chemistry Recipes
  • You may read Level 1 Science TAGS, and undertake Science TASKS
Level 2
  • You know 6 Chemistry Recipes
  • You may read Level 1 and 2 Science TAGS, and undertake Science TASKS
Level 3
  • You know 10 Chemistry Recipes
  • You may read level 1,2 and 3 Science TAGS, and undertake Science TASKS

Pharmacology Talents

Additional Recipes (1 skill point)

You know 2 additional recipes each time you take this skill.

This talent may be taken once per level of Pharmacology skill you have.

Monarch Reagent (1 skill point)

You undertake Science TASK bead pulls at advantage.

Heisenberg (1 skill point)

Your Narcotics are potent. Each will last 1 minute longer per level of Pharmacology you have.

Pharmaceutical engineer (1 skill point)

The effectiveness of any medicines you brew are increased.

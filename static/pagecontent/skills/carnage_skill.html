Carnage

Archetype Skill

You are highly trained in the arts of breaking the human body, and stopping people from breaking yours. Many of these principles carry over very effectively when used against near-human and non-human foes.

The Carnage skill allows you to dish out some serious damage and gives you access to melee weapon calls that can make you even more effective in combat. It can also make you tankier, allowing you to withstand harm in combat, and synergises well with the D-Fens, Never Say Die, and True Grit skills.

Level 1
  • You have 1 extra point of GRIT
Level 2
  • You have another 1 extra point of GRIT (cumulative with Carnage level 1)
Level 3
  • You have yet another 1 extra point of GRIT (cumulative with Carnage levels 1 and 2)

Carnage Talents

Smashy Weapons (1 Skill point)

You can use Smashy Weapons.

Pokey Weapons (1 Skill point)

You can use Pokey Weapons.

Street Sweepers (1 Skill point)

You can use Street Sweeper firearms.

Heavy Weapons (1 Skill point)

You can use Heavy Weapon firearms.

Disarm (1 Skill point)

Upon striking your opponent's weapon or arm, you may spend 1 point of GRIT to call “DISARM”. Your target must drop the item/weapon hit or held. (If the weapon is a firearm it is JAMMED instead. A JAM can be cleared with 30 seconds of appropriate roleplay). (Fragile or precious physreps may be held behind your back for 30 seconds if dropping them would not be safe or practical). Your GRIT is not spent if the target does not react accordingly.

Strike Down (1 Skill point)

Upon striking your opponent's legs or torso, you may spend 1 point of GRIT to call “STRIKE-DOWN”. Your target must then fall to the floor (their buttocks or shoulders must touch the ground). Your GRIT is not spent if the target does not react accordingly.

MAIM (1 Skill point)

Upon striking your opponent, you may spend 1 point of GRIT to call “MAIM”. This inflicts 1 point of GRIEVOUS HARM which your opponent cannot absorb by spending GRIT. NPCs should show immediate sign of having suffered a Consequence; your GRIT is not spent if the target does not react accordingly.

Applied with a Trowel (1 Skill point)

War Paint provides you with an additional 3 GRIT rather than 2.

Polish your Brass (1 Skill point)

You start each event with 1 additional Ammo per level of Carnage.

Firearm Handling (1 skill point)

If your gun is JAMMED (e.g. by a DISARM call), you may spend 1 GRIT and 10 seconds of appropriate roleplaying (instead of the normal 30 seconds) to clear the JAM.

Way of the Sword (1 skill point)

Once per day you may use a call with a Hand Weapon without having to spend GRIT.

Way of the Hammer (1 skill point)

Once per day you may use a call with a Smashy Weapon without having to spend GRIT.

Way of the Spear (1 skill point)

Once per day you may use a call with a Pokey Weapon without having to spend GRIT.

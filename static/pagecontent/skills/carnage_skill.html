Carnage

Archetype Skill

You are highly trained in the arts of breaking the human body, and stopping people from breaking yours. Many of these principles carry over very effectively when used against near human and non human foes.

Level 1
  • You have 1 extra point of GRIT
Level 2
  • You have 2 extra points of GRIT (not cumulative with level 1)
Level 3
  • You have 3 extra points of GRIT (not cumulative with level 1 and 2)

Carnage Talents

Scavenger (1 skill point)

Each time you come out of the Hot Zone you will have gathered one random resource per level of the Scout skill you have.

Picklock (1 skill point per level)

Your levels in Picklock cannot exceed your levels in the base Scout skill

You can undertake Lock Pick TASKS.

Evaluate (1 skill point)

You can open a Resources case to see what is inside whilst inside a Hot Zone. You may not take (or interact with) the contents until the container is opened normally.

Note that this essentially lets you peek into a container to ascertain if it is worth spending the time and resources necessary to open it properly.

Applied with a Trowel (1 skill point)

Your Warpaint provides you an additional 1 GRIT.

Street Sweepers (1 Skill point)

You can use Street Sweeper firearms.

Dirty fighting (1 skill point)

You may use this ability twice per day. If your opponent is “down” (i.e. neither foot flat on the floor), and you are using a weapon under 24” in you can spend 1 point of GRIT to call “MAIM”. This inflicts 1 point of GRIEVOUS HARM which the opponent cannot spend GRIT to avoid. (NPCs should show immediate sign of having suffered a consequence - if they do not then you retain the GRIT you would have spent).

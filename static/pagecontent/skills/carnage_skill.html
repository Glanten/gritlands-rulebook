Carnage

Archetype Skill

You are highly trained in the arts of breaking the human body, and stopping people from breaking yours. Many of these principles carry over very effectively when used against near human and non human foes.

Level 1
  • You have 1 extra point of GRIT
Level 2
  • You have 2 extra points of GRIT (not cumulative with level 1)
Level 3
  • You have 3 extra points of GRIT (not cumulative with level 1 and 2)

Carnage Talents

Smashy Weapons (1 Skill point)

You Can use Smashy Weapons.

Pokey Weapons (1 Skill point)

You can use Pokey Weapons.

Street Sweepers (1 Skill point)

You can use Street Sweeper firearms.

Heavy Weapons (1 Skill point)

You can use Heavy Weapon firearms.

Disarm (1 Skill point)

When you strike your opponent's weapon or arm, you can spend 1 point of GRIT to call “DISARM”. Your target must drop the item/weapon hit or held. (If the weapon is a firearm it instead JAMS. You can clear a JAM with 30 seconds of appropriate roleplay). (Other fragile physreps can be held behind your back for 30 seconds if dropping them would not be safe). If the target does not you retain the GRIT you would have spent.

Strike Down (1 Skill point)

When you strike your opponents Legs or Torso, you can spend 1 point of GRIT to call “STRIKEDOWN”. Your target must then fall to the floor (their rear or shoulders must touch the ground). (If they do not then you retain the GRIT you would have spent).

MAIM (1 Skill point)

When you strike your opponent, you can spend 1 point of GRIT to call “MAIM”. This inflicts 1 point of GRIEVOUS HARM which the opponent cannot spend GRIT to absorb. (NPCs should show immediate sign of having suffered a consequence - if they do not then you retain the GRIT you would have spent)

Applied with a Trowel (1 Skill point)

Warpaint provides 2 GRIT rather than 1.

Polish your Brass (1 Skill point)

You start each event with 1 additional Ammo per level of Carnage.

Firearm Handling (1 skill point)

If your gun jams, spend 1 grit to clear it in 10 seconds rather than 30.

Way of the Sword (1 skill point)

Once per day you can use a call with a Hand Weapon without having to spend GRIT.

Way of the Hammer (1 skill point)

Once per day you can use a call with a Smashy Weapon without having to spend GRIT.

Way of the Spear (1 skill point)

Once per day you can use a call with a Pokey Weapon without having to spend GRIT.

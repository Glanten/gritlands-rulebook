...& Chewing Gum

Everypony

There are all kinds of things that survivors in the Gritlands can do that don't fall into the neat categories of skills. This skill is the catch-all that every character has that serves to cover those kinds of things.

The ...& Chewing Gum skill has no direct mechanical effects, but each level provides access to certain Talents or increases the effectiveness of other skills.

Level 1

Your character exists, breaths, dreams. They can read and count and use their eyes to see things. They are able to carry and even use items in both hands at once (yes, even weapons). Walking is probable, likely even...

Level 2

You have grown as a person.

Level 3

You are totally self aware, and absolutely a real person, you do not live in a simulation...

...& Chewing Gum Talents

Pipe Guns (1 Skill point)

You can use Pipe Gun Firearms.

Hand Weapon (1 Skill point)

You can use Hand Weapons.

War Paint (1 Skill point)

When wearing Warpaint, you receive 2 extra points of GRIT.

Bows (1 Skill point)

You can use Bows and Crossbows.

Warning: Although some may mock their lack of effectiveness, using a LARP-safe crossbow (or two LARP-safe crossbows, one in each hand) at GRITLANDS ups your cool factor by a near-infinite value.

Thrown Weapons (1 Skill point)

You can use Thrown Weapons.

Pistols (1 Skill point)

You can use Pistol Firearms.

“The first (mutagen) is free...” (0 skill points)

The player must display a clear cosmetic mutation. This should be obvious and unmistakable. Check the Mutations rules for more information.

Strong (1 Skill Point)

May be taken once per level of ...& Chewing Gum

You may count your Strength level against GRIT expenditure for certain TASKS (breaking things or carrying things, the details will be clear from the TASK description).

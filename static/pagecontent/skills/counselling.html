Counselling

Everypony

The Gritlands aren't just physically threatening and demanding. They take their toll on your mind as well. Those with the empathy, and the strength to support others are essential in driving society forward in the face of adversity. Without them nihilism and despair would all too easily quench human (and near human) spirit.

Level 1
  • Once per day you can Treat a Minor (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Minor consequences each day for 1 GRIT each.
  • For 2 points of GRIT you can treat a Moderate (Psyche) consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can treat a Major (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled, the consequence is not stabilised and will continue to worsen towards the CRITICAL).
  • You cannot use this skill to treat your own consequences.
Level 2
  • Twice per day you can Treat a Minor (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Minor consequences each day for 1 GRIT each.
  • Once per day you can treat a Moderate(Psyche) consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Moderate consequences each day for 2 GRIT each.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can treat a Major (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled, the consequence is not stabilised and will continue to worsen towards the TERMINAL). You may use this skill to treat one of your own Minor consequences for 2 additional GRIT (you cannot treat your own consequences for free).
Level 3
  • Thrice per day you can Treat a Minor (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Minor consequences each day for 1 GRIT each.
  • Twice per day you can treat a Moderate (Psyche) consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Moderate consequences each day for 2 GRIT each.
  • Once per day you can treat a Major (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled, the consequence is not stabilised and will continue to worsen towards the TERMINAL).
  • You can treat additional Major consequences each day for 3 GRIT each.
  • You may use this skill to treat either one of your own Minor consequences for 2 additional GRIT or one of your own Moderate consequences for 3 additional GRIT (you cannot treat your own consequences for free).

Counselling Talents

Take your own Advice (1 skill point)

Once per day you can treat a minor consequence you have suffered without spending GRIT.

Popping Pills (1 skill point)

Requires the First Aid skill.

You can treat one additional Minor Consequence each day without spending GRIT while working with a first aid kit focus.

Chill Pill (1 skill point)

Requires the First Aid skill.

You can treat one additional Moderate Consequence each day without spending GRIT while working with a first aid kit focus.

Hey! It's me, it's me! (1 skill point)

If the player you are treating is a member of your group you spend 1 less GRIT to treat them (to a minimum of 0).

Ask for Help (1 skill point)

If there is a player from your group to roleplay with you can treat 1 Minor consequence you are suffering by talking to them as the appropriate roleplay, reducing the extra GRIT cost to only 1.

Self Help Group (1 skill point)

You can treat 1 extra player per level of Counselling that you have if they are suffering a Minor or Moderate consequences from the same cause. Additionally, if you also have the 'Ask for Help' Talent you may treat yourself. You must spend 1 extra minute per player, but do not need to spend additional GRIT.

Practised Patter (3 points)

Requires the Counselling skill at level 3.

Up to 3 times per day you may reduce your GRIT expenditure when using this skill by 1 to a minimum of 1.

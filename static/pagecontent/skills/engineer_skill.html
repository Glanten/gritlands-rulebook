Engineer Skill

Archetype, Professional

You can make things, repair things, maintain things. You have hard won and ancient knowledge relating to mechanics, electronics, architecture, material sciences... even engines, computers, programming, and more!

Skill Focus: Tool kit and/or workbench.

Engineering allows you to craft items by combining raw materials (normally recovered from a HOTZONE) and blueprints (that your character has learned or researched). It also allows you to interact with electrical and mechanical elements in the world, which is a very valuable skill to have in HOTZONES that were once functional facilities from the beforetimes.

Level 1
  • You know 3 Engineering recipes
  • You generate 2 maintenance TAGS at the start of each event
Level 2
  • As Level 1 plus:
  • You know 3 more Engineering recipes
  • You generate 2 additional maintenance TAGS at the start of each event
Level 3
  • As Level 2 plus:
  • You know an additional 4 Engineering recipes
  • You generate 3 more maintenance TAGS at the start of each event

Engineering Talents

Additional Recipes (1 skill point)

You may take this talent once for every level of the Engineering skill you have.

Every time you take this talent, you gain 2 additional Engineering recipes.

Scrap hoarder (1 Skill point)

You spend 1 less Scrap per level of the recipe you are working on.

Mechanical Genius (1 Skill point)

You make Engineering TASK bead pulls at advantage (pull twice and take the better result).

Maintainer (1 skill point)

This talent may be taken a maximum of 3 times.

You generate 1 additional maintenance TAG at the start of every event.

Recycle (1 skill point)

With 2 minutes of appropriate roleplay, you may break down a THING in order to reclaim some resources used in its construction. The THING'S lammie should contain the relevant information. This process destroys the original item.

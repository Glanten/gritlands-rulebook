Engineer Skill

Archetype, Professional

You can make things, repair things, maintain things. You have hard won and ancient knowledge relating to mechanics, electronics, architecture, material sciences... Even computers, programs and more.

FOCUS: Tool kit and/or workbench.

Level 1
  • You know 3 Engineering Recipes
  • You generate 2 maintenance TAGS at the start of each event
  • You may read Level 1 Engineering TAGS, and undertake Engineering TASKS
Level 2
  • You know 6 Engineering Recipes
  • You generate 4 maintenance TAGS at the start of each event
  • You may read Level 1 and 2 engineering TAGS, and undertake Engineering TASKS
Level 3
  • You know 10 Engineering Recipes
  • You generate 7 additional maintenance TAGS at the start of each event
  • You may read level 1, 2 and 3 Engineering TAGS, and undertake Engineering TASKS

Engineering Talents

Additional Recipes (1 skill point)

May be taken once per Level of Engineering skill.

You know 2 additional recipes each time you take this skill.

Scrap hoarder (1 Skill point)

You spend 1 less Scrap per level of the recipe you are working on.

Mechanical Genius (1 Skill point)

Engineering TASK bead pulls are made at advantage.

Maintainer (1 skill point)

May be taken up to 3 times.

You generate 1 additional Maintenance tag.

Recycle (1 skill point)

2 minutes roleplay allows you to break down a THING in order to reclaim some resources used in its construction for other purposes. The lammie will contain the relevant information. The original item is destroyed in this process.

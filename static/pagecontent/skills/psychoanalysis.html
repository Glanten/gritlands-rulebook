Psychoanalysis

Everypony

If we were but unfeeling sacks of flesh we'd feel much more at home in the Hot Zones: but alas, we see, we feel - we remember. Understanding, accepting and mastering the horrors of the Gritlands isn't merely a 'nice to have' - it's a necessity.

Skill Focus: A therapy room including two seats, privacy, and (relative) quiet.

Psychoanalysis is very similar to the Medic skill but it heals mental maladies instead of physical injuries. HOTZONE hazards are commonly physical (radiation, nanites, traps, mutants, toxic gas, etc.) but explorers are often stumble from such places beset by stress, fear, despair, and weird psychological effects that no amount of bandages can fix.

Level 1
  • You may spend 1 GRIT and engage in 2 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to treat a Minor (Psyche) Consequence. This treatment removes the Consequence entirely from the patient. There is no GRIT cost for this skill the first time you use it on a given day.
  • You may spend 2 GRIT and engage in 5 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to treat a Moderate (Psyche) Consequence. This treatment removes the Consequence entirely from the patient.
  • You may spend 3 GRIT and engage in 5 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to treat a Major (Psyche) Consequence, so long as it has been stabilised. This treatment removes the Consequence entirely from the patient.
  • You may spend 3 GRIT and attempt to stabilise a Major (Psyche) Consequence with a series of bead-pulls. 3 successful bead-pulls stabilises the Consequence, whereas 3 unsuccessful bead-pulls worsens the condition to a Critical Consequence. You must engage in 1 minute of appropriate roleplaying between each bead-pull.
  • You may spend 3 GRIT and attempt to stabilise a Critical (Psyche) Consequence with a series of bead-pulls. 3 successful bead-pulls stabilises the Consequence, whereas 3 unsuccessful bead-pulls will inflict a Terminal condition on the character (at this point their fate is sealed and there is nothing more you can do for them). You must engage in 1 minute of appropriate roleplaying between each bead-pull.
  • You may not use this skill to treat your own Consequences.
Level 2
  • As level 1 plus:
  • You gain an additional free daily use of this skill to treat Minor (Psyche) Consequences.
  • You gain a free daily use of this skill to treat a Moderate (Psyche) Consequence.
  • You still may not use this skill to treat your own Consequences.
Level 3
  • As level 2 plus:
  • You gain another free daily use of this skill to treat Minor (Psyche) Consequences.
  • You gain a second free daily use of this skill to treat a Moderate (Psyche) Consequence.
  • Once per day you may perform one of the following actions with the normal time and roleplaying requirements, but with no GRIT cost or bead-pull:
    • Treat a stabilised Major (Psyche) Consequence
    • Stabilise a Major (Psyche) Consequence
    • Stabilise a Critical (Psyche) Consequence
  • Without a special talent that lets you ignore this rule, there is no way to use this skill to heal your own Consequences.

Psychoanalysis Talents

Cognitive Ball Therapy (1 skill point)

You may treat your own Minor (Psyche) Consequences in the same way as you would treat another character, but the base cost of treating your own Consequence is 2 GRIT and you may not use a free daily use of this skill to treat yourself.

Pep Talk (1 skill point)

You can spend 1 point of GRIT per treatment to eschew the need for a therapy room as a Skill Focus.

Any bead-pulls performed when using this talent are made at disadvantage (draw twice and keep the worse result).

Calming Voice (1 skill point)

All Psychoanalysis bead-pulls you perform are made at advantage (draw twice and keep the better result).

Empathy (1 skill point)

Reduce the cost of healing another character's Psyche Consequence by 1 GRIT.

Psychosomatic (1 skill point)

You can use Psychoanalysis to heal Minor (Physical) Consequences in the same way you would heal Minor (Psyche) Consequences.

Psychobabble (1 skill point)

Once per day, with 5 minutes of bewildering jargon, you may restore GRIT to another player of an amount up to your Psychoanalysis level.

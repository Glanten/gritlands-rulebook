Psychoanalysis

Everypony

If we were but unfeeling sacks of flesh we'd feel much more at home in the Hot Zones: but alas, we see, we feel - we remember. Understanding, accepting and mastering the horrors of the Gritlands isn't merely a 'nice to have' - it's a necessity.

Focus: Therapy room. 2 seats, privacy and quiet.

Note: You cannot heal your own Consequences with this skill!

Level 1
  • Once Per Day you can Heal a minor (Psyche) consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional Minor consequences for 1 GRIT.
  • For 2 points of GRIT you can Heal a Moderate (Psyche) consequence with 5 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can HEAL a Stabilised Major (Psyche) Consequence with 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a Major (Psyche) Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to gain a CRITICAL consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a CRITICAL (Psyche) Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to TERMINAL. There is nothing more you can do for them. Smh.
  • You can read level 1 Psychoanalysis and Profiling TAGs and undertake Psychoanalysis and Profiling TASKS.
Level 2
  • Twice Per Day you can Heal a minor (Psyche) Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional minor Consequences for 1 GRIT.
  • Once per day you can Heal a Moderate (Psyche) consequence with 5 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional moderate Consequences for 2 GRIT.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can HEAL a Stabilised Major (Psyche) Consequence with 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional Major Consequences for 3 GRIT.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a Major (Psyche) Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to gain a CRITICAL consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a CRITICAL (Psyche) Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to TERMINAL. There is nothing more you can do for them. Smh.
  • You can read level 1 and 2 Psychoanalysis and Profiling TAGs and undertake Psychoanalysis and Profiling TASKS.
Level 3
  • Thrice Per Day you can Heal a minor (Psyche) cConsequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional minor Consequences for 1 GRIT.
  • Twice per day you can Heal a Moderate (Psyche) Consequence with 5 minutes of roleplay. The consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional Moderate Consequences for 2 GRIT.
  • Once per day you can HEAL a Stabilised Major (Psyche) Consequence with 5 minutes of roleplay. The Consequence is removed.
  • You may heal additional Major Consequences for 3 GRIT.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a Major (Psyche) Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to gain a CRITICAL consequence.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can attempt to Stabilise a CRITICAL (Psyche) Consequence. This takes 1 minute per Bead pull. 3 Successes required to Stabilise the consequence. 3 Failures cause the Character to move to TERMINAL. There is nothing more you can do for them. Smh.
  • You can read level 1, 2 and 3 Psychoanalysis and Profiling TAGs and undertake Psychoanalysis and Profiling TASKS.

Psychoanalysis Talents

Cognitive Ball Therapy (1 skill point)

You can heal your own Minor Consequences for 2 GRIT.

Pep Talk (1 skill point)

For 1 additional point of GRIT you can HEAL Minor, Moderate Consequences or Stabilise Major or Critical Consequences without a Therapy room Focus by taking the bead pull with disadvantage.

Calming Voice (1 skill point)

When using Psychoanalysis you get an advantage on any associated bead pull.

Empathy (1 skill point)

When using Psychoanalysis you get an advantage on any associated bead pull.

Psychosomatic (1 skill point)

You can use Psychoanalysis to heal Minor physical consequences.

Psychobabble (1 skill point)

Once per day, with 5 minutes of bewildering jargon, you can restore GRIT to another player of an amount up to your Psychoanalyst level.

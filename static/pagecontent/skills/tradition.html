Tradition

Archetype

This skill varies by clan.

Tea Ceremony

NuGov Ritual

You have warmed the Pot. The water has been heated to the correct temperature. You will be mum. You have the deeper understanding of ISO 3103 and can lead the tea ceremony for your fellows.

This requires 15 minutes of roleplay.

Each participant regains GRIT equal to your Reputation.

Level 1
  • Once per day you can lead the tea ceremony for (Reputation x 2) +1 NuGuv citizens.
Level 2
  • Twice per day you can lead the tea ceremony for (Reputation x 2) +2 NuGuv citizens.
Level 3
  • Thrice per day you can lead the tea ceremony for (Reputation x 2) +3 NuGuv citizens.

Tea Ceremony Talents

Pre-warmed pot (1 skill point)

The tea ceremony only takes 10 minutes when you perform it.

“Another round” (3 skill points)

You can perform the ceremony and additional time each day.

One for the Pot (1 skill point)

You regain GRIT along with the participants when you perform the ceremony.

Strong and Sweet (3 skill points)

Each participant regains 1 additional GRIT.

Witness

Wastelander Ritual

You have chronicled the Heroes, now you must tell their story.

NOTE: Any Wastelander player may call attention to their deeds and action with an appropriate shout of “Watch this!” “Observe” “Get a load of this!” Or even “Witness!” It is up to an Enabler to respond. The Response is based on the Enabler's Reputation level.

Reputation 1: “Camera ONE rolling” - the Wastelander who called for your attention regains 1 GRIT.
Reputation 2: “Ready for a TWO Shot” - the Wastelander who called for your attention regains 2 GRIT.
Reputation 3: “Prying open my THIRD Eye” - the Wastelander who called for your attention regains 3 GRIT.

Level 1
  • Respond: five times per day you can Respond according to the description above.
  • Replay: Once per day (not in the Hot Zone) you may spend 5 minutes recounting the deeds you have witnessed that day. Every wastelander who stops to listen to the entire story regains 1 point of GRIT. Any wastelander who you witnessed and are telling the tale about regains an additional point of GRIT.
Level 2
  • Respond: ten times per day you can Respond as described above.
  • Replay: Twice per day (not in the Hot Zone) you may spend 5 minutes recounting the deeds you have witnessed that day. Every wastelander who stops to listen to the entire story regains 1 point of GRIT. Any wastelander who you witnessed and are telling the tale about regains an additional point of GRIT.
Level 3
  • Respond: fifteen times per day you can Respond according to the description above.
  • Replay: Thrice per day (not in the Hot Zone) you may spend 5 minutes recounting the deeds you have witnessed that day. Every wastelander who stops to listen to the entire story regains 1 point of GRIT. Any wastelander who you witnessed and are telling the tale about regains an additional point of GRIT.

Record, Rewind, Replay Talents

Cohost (1 skill point)

You can assist another Broadcaster in their recounting. They must lead for at least 3 of the 5 minutes. Your reputation cannot be higher than theirs. You spend 3 GRIT. You double the GRIT regained by the audience.

Live at the Scene (1 skill point)

Once per day you may Use Replay in the Hot Zone.

Oaths

Knights of the Realm Ritual

You record, and adjudicate the completion of Oaths.

NOTE: No Knights of the Realm character may have more active oaths than the higher of their …&Chewing Gum or Reputation skill.

Knights may swear Oaths to you. It is up to you to determine that they are suitable oaths. If they are, you record them in the book of Oaths. The player that swore the oath gains GRIT equal to your Reputation.

Level 1
  • Once per day you may announce that up to (Reputation) oaths have been fulfilled. Each fulfilled oath grants its swearer your REPUTATION plus their Reputation in GRIT, and the same again that they may bestow upon their (Knights) comrades (comrades must have been present at the completion of the oath, or instrumental in its completion) at no more than 1 point per comrade.
Level 2
  • Twice per day you may announce that up to (Reputation) oaths have been fulfilled. Each fulfilled oath grants its swearer your REPUTATION plus their Reputation in GRIT, and the same again that they may bestow upon their (Knights) comrades (comrades must have been present at the completion of the oath, or instrumental in its completion) at no more than 1 point per comrade.
Level 3
  • Thrice per day you may announce that up to (Reputation) oaths have been fulfilled. Each fulfilled oath grants its swearer your REPUTATION plus their Reputation in GRIT, and the same again that they may bestow upon their (Knights) comrades (comrades must have been present at the completion of the oath, or instrumental in its completion) at no more than 1 point per comrade.

Pronouncement of Oaths Talents

Glory, Glory! (1 skill point)

Once per day, upon witnessing the completion of an oath sworn to you in a Hot Zone, you may immediately announce the oath's completion. This does not count towards your daily announcement limit.

In Memoriam (1 skill point)

If a character fulfils an oath sworn by a now deceased member of their group you may choose to bestow the GRIT reward upon them as if they originally swore the oath.

Catechism

Unnumbered Host Ritual

You are trenchant in your faith, crystal clear in your vision, and LOUD in your words.

Once all sermons/challenges have been completed every attending character who is part of your group regains your Reputation value in GRIT. Every attending Host character also gains 1 additional GRIT for each preacher who was involved.

Level 1
  • Once per day you can deliver a 5 minute sermon on the truth of your specific religious beliefs. Another Preacher may challenge you with a 5 minute response (and another challenge them etc.)
Level 2
  • Twice per day you can deliver a 5 minute sermon on the truth of your specific religious beliefs. Another Preacher may challenge you with a 5 minute response (and another challenge them etc.)
Level 3
  • Thrice per day you can deliver a 5 minute sermon on the truth of your specific religious beliefs. Another Preacher may challenge you with a 5 minute response (and another challenge them etc.)

Catechism Talents

Staff of Office (1 skill point)

You have the ability to wield one specific smashy or pokey weapon (see participant handbook). That weapon must take the form of a religious icon appropriate for your character's beliefs. You may change the specific weapon by performing a 5 minute rite (prayers, meditation, chanting, etc).

Syncretist (1 skill point)

Once per day you may consider another group to be part of your group when restoring GRIT from a sermon.

Populist (1 skill point)

Once per day you may perform an additional sermon - this sermon only restores 1 GRIT for each participating preacher to attending characters.

Chaplain (1 skill point)

Once per day one of your sermons may be conducted inside the Hot Zone.

I Kick Ass For The Great Leader (1 Skill Point)

When you are unable to deliver any more sermons for a day you gain Reputation GRIT (this may temporarily increase you GRIT above is usual maximum level).

Verbose (1 skill point)

You may perform one additional Sermon per day.

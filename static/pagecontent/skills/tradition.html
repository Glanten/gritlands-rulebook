Tradition

Archetype

This skill varies by clan.

Tea Ceremony

NuGov Tradition

You have warmed the Pot. The water has been heated to the correct temperature. You will be mum. You have the deeper understanding of ISO 3103 and can lead the tea ceremony for your fellows.

A Tea Ceremony requires 15 minutes of roleplay.

When you are leading a Tea Ceremony, the default maximum participants is equal to twice your Reputation. You can involve an extra participant for each level of Tea Ceremony you have (e.g. if you have Reputation 2 and Tea Ceremony 1, you can hold a ceremony for 5 people).

Each participant of the Tea Ceremony, but not the person leading it, regains GRIT equal to your Reputation.

Level 1

You can lead a Tea Ceremony once per day.

Level 2

You can lead a Tea Ceremony twice per day.

Level 3

You can lead a Tea Ceremony three times per day.

Tea Ceremony Talents

Pre-warmed pot (1 skill point)

The Tea Ceremony only takes 10 minutes when you perform it.

Another Round (3 skill points)

You can perform one more Tea Ceremony per day.

One for the Pot (1 skill point)

You regain GRIT along with the participants when you perform the Tea Ceremony.

Strong and Sweet (3 skill points)

Each participant regains an extra 1 GRIT.

Witness

Wastelander Tradition

You have chronicled the Heroes, now you must tell their story.

Sometimes known as "Record, Rewind, Replay", this tradition has two parts: responding to a call to witness, and recounting the deeds you have witnesses.

Any Wastelander character may call for other Wastelanders to witness their deeds and actions with an appropriate shout of “Watch this!” “Observe” “Get a load of this!” Or even “Witness!” If an Enabler responds appropriately, the character who called for witnesses regains GRIT equal to the Enabler's Reputation level (see below).

Reputation 1: “Camera ONE rolling” - the Wastelander who called for your attention regains 1 GRIT.

Reputation 2: “Ready for a TWO Shot” - the Wastelander who called for your attention regains 2 GRIT.

Reputation 3: “Prying open my THIRD Eye” - the Wastelander who called for your attention regains 3 GRIT.

To recount the deeds you have witnessed, you may spend 5 minutes of appropriate roleplaying to narrate the deeds you have been called to witness. Every Wastelander character who stops to listen to the entire story (cheering, pumping their fists, slapping their thighs, etc.) regains 1 GRIT. If the Wastelander you are telling the story about is present, that individual gains an extra 1 GRIT. This retelling may not be performed in the HOTZONE.

Level 1

You can respond to a maximum of 5 calls to witness per day.

You can recount one witnessed deed per day.

Level 2

You can respond to a maximum of 10 calls to witness per day.

You can recount an extra witnessed deed every day.

Level 3

You can respond to a maximum of 15 calls to witness per day.

You can recount yet another witnessed deed every day.

Witness Talents

Cohost (1 skill point)

By spending 3 GRIT, you can assist another Broadcaster in their recounting. They must lead for the majority of the roleplaying and your Reputation may not be higher than theirs. Any GRIT regained by the audience is doubled.

Live at the Scene (1 skill point)

Once per day you may use Witness in the HOTZONE.

Oaths

Knights of the Realm Tradition

You may record and adjudicate the completion of Oaths.

Knights may swear Oaths to you. It is up to you to determine that they are suitable oaths. If they are, you may record them in the Book of Oaths. The character that swore the oath gains GRIT equal to your Reputation.

No Knights of the Realm character may have more active oaths than the higher of their ...& Chewing Gum or Reputation skill.

When you announce that an Oath has been fulfilled (see below), the swearer of that Oath gains GRIT equal to the sum of your Reputation and their Reputation. They may then grant the same amount of GRIT to their fellow Knights (maximum 1 GRIT per knight) who were present at, or instrumental to, the completion of that Oath.

Level 1

Once per day, you may announce that Oaths have been fulfilled. The number of Oaths announced must not exceed your Reputation skill.

Level 2

Twice per day, you may announce that Oaths have been fulfilled. Each time, the number of Oaths announced must not exceed your Reputation skill.

Level 3

Thrice per day, you may announce that Oaths have been fulfilled. Each time, the number of Oaths announced must not exceed your Reputation skill.

Pronouncement of Oaths Talents

Glory, Glory! (1 skill point)

Once per day, upon witnessing the completion of an Oath sworn to you in a HOTZONE, you may immediately announce the Oath's completion. This does not count towards your daily announcement limit.

In Memoriam (1 skill point)

If a character fulfils an Oath sworn by a now-deceased member of their Group, you may choose to bestow the GRIT reward upon them as if they originally swore the Oath.

Catechism

Unnumbered Host Tradition

You are trenchant in your faith, crystal clear in your vision, and LOUD in your words.

Delivering a sermon requires 5 minutes of appropriate roleplaying, delivering the truth of your specific beliefs. Another Preacher may then challenge your sermon with their own 5 minute response; they may, in turn, be challenged by another Preacher, and so on.

Once all sermons and challenges have been completed, every attending character who is part of your Group regains GRIT equal to your Reputation. Every attending Host character (inlcuding characters from your Group) also gains 1 GRIT for every preacher who was involved in the sermons and challenges.

Unless stated otherwise, sermons and challenges may not be performed in the HOTZONE.

Level 1

You can deliver one sermon per day. You may make as many challenges to another Preacher's sermons as you wish.

Level 2

You can deliver two sermons per day. You may make as many challenges to another Preacher's sermons as you wish.

Level 3

You can deliver three sermon per day! You may make as many challenges to another Preacher's sermons as you wish.

Catechism Talents

Staff of Office (1 skill point)

You have the ability to wield a unique Smashy or Pokey weapon that you otherwise would not be able to use. That weapon must take the form of a religious icon appropriate for your character's beliefs. You may change the specific weapon by performing a 5 minute rite (prayers, meditation, chanting, etc).

Syncretist (1 skill point)

Once per day, you may consider another Group to be part of your Group when restoring GRIT from a sermon.

Populist (1 skill point)

Once per day, you may perform an additional sermon of lesser effectiveness: this sermon only restores 1 GRIT for each participating preacher to attending characters.

Chaplain (1 skill point)

You may conduct one of your daily sermons in the HOTZONE.

I Kick Ass For The Great Leader (1 Skill Point)

When you perform your last daily sermon, you gain GRIT equal to your Reputation. This may temporarily raise your GRIT above its normal maximum.

Verbose (1 skill point)

You may perform one additional sermon per day.

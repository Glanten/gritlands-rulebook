Scout Skill

Archetype, Professional

You can read the signs in the wind, the taste on the air, identify the flora and fauna of the Gritlands and you know where it thrives. You can read the tell tale signs left behind by the occupants and inhabitants of an area, you can follow tracks and identify the debris and patterns of many Gritlands Denizens.

FOCI equipment - Binoculars, Environmental survey device, Theodolite, dowsing rod, jars of dirt / herbs / moss / guano etc. As reference material. Ordnance survey maps (incomplete, heavily annotated).

Level 1
  • Twice per day you can avoid HARM inflicted by the environment without spending GRIT
  • You may read only Level 1 Scouting TAGS, and undertake Scouting TASKS
  • Twice per day you can avoid GRIEVOUS HARM inflicted by the environment by spending one GRIT
Level 2
  • Thrice per day you can avoid HARM inflicted by the environment without spending GRIT
  • You may read only Level 1 and 2 Scouting TAGS, and undertake Scouting TASKS
  • Thrice per day you can avoid GRIEVOUS HARM inflicted by the environment by spending one GRIT
Level 3
  • Four times per day you can avoid HARM inflicted by the environment without spending GRIT
  • You may read only Level 1, 2 and 3 Scouting TAGS, and undertake Scouting TASKS
  • Four times per day you can avoid GRIEVOUS HARM inflicted by the environment by spending one GRIT

Scout Talents

Scavenger (1 skill point)

Each time you come out of the Hot Zone you will have gathered one random resource per level of the Scout skill you have.

Picklock (1 skill point per level)

Your levels in Picklock cannot exceed your levels in the base Scout skill

You can undertake Lock Pick TASKS.

Evaluate (1 skill point)

You can open a Resources case to see what is inside whilst inside a Hot Zone. You may not take (or interact with) the contents until the container is opened normally.

Note that this essentially lets you peek into a container to ascertain if it is worth spending the time and resources necessary to open it properly.

Applied with a Trowel (1 skill point)

Your Warpaint provides you an additional 1 GRIT.

Street Sweepers (1 Skill point)

You can use Street Sweeper firearms.

Dirty fighting (1 skill point)

You may use this ability twice per day. If your opponent is “down” (i.e. neither foot flat on the floor), and you are using a weapon under 24” in you can spend 1 point of GRIT to call “MAIM”. This inflicts 1 point of GRIEVOUS HARM which the opponent cannot spend GRIT to avoid. (NPCs should show immediate sign of having suffered a consequence - if they do not then you retain the GRIT you would have spent).

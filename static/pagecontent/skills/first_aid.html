First Aid

Everypony

The Gritlands are tough: injury and hazard are constant companions. So it is unsurprising that you have learned how to patch up quite serious wounds, at least enough to get back to safety.

FOCUS: The first aid skill requires you to have an appropriate physrep first aid kit. This kit must be used as part of your appropriate roleplaying.

Level 1
  • Once per day you can Treat a Minor Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Minor consequences each day for 1 GRIT each.
  • For 2 points of GRIT you can treat a Moderate consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can treat a Major Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled, the consequence is not stabilised and will continue to worsen towards the CRITICAL).
  • You may treat your own Minor Consequences at the cost of 1 additional GRIT (you cannot treat your own consequences for free).
Level 2
  • Twice per day you can Treat a Minor Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Minor consequences each day for 1 GRIT each.
  • Once per day you can treat a Moderate consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Moderate consequences each day for 2 GRIT each.
  • For 3 points of GRIT you can treat a Major Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled, the consequence is not stabilised and will continue to worsen towards the TERMINAL).
  • You may treat your own Moderate Consequences for 2 additional GRIT or your own Minor Consequences for 1 additional GRIT (you cannot treat your own consequences for free).
Level 3
  • Thrice per day you can Treat a Minor Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Minor consequences each day for 1 GRIT each.
  • Twice per day you can treat a Moderate consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled).
  • You can treat additional Moderate consequences each day for 2 GRIT each.
  • Once per day you can treat a Major Consequence with 2 minutes of roleplay. The character can ignore the negative effects of the consequence for 30 minutes (the consequence slot remains filled, the consequence is not stabilised and will continue to worsen towards the TERMINAL).
  • You can treat additional Major consequences each day for 3 grit each.
  • You may treat your own Major Consequences for 3 additional GRIT, your own Moderate Consequences for 2 additional GRIT or your own Minor Consequences for 1 additional GRIT (you cannot treat your own consequences for free).

First Aid Talents

Extra Bandage (1 skill point)

You can treat one additional Minor Consequence on another character each day without spending GRIT.

Strong Painkillers (1 skill point)

You can treat one additional Moderate Consequence on another character each day without spending GRIT.

Vitamin -K (1 skill point)

You can treat one additional Major Consequence on another character each day without spending GRIT.

A Taste of my own medicine (1 skill point)

Once per day you can treat a minor consequence you have suffered without spending GRIT.

Improvised Splint (1 skill point)

Once per Hotzone Visit you can treat a minor or moderate consequence without needing a First Aid kit.

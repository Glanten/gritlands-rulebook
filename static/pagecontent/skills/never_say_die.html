Never Say Die

Everypony

You. Just. Won't. DIE!

Consequences aren't always acquired from facing mortal danger, but run out of Consequences you die; this skill gives you more Consequences, hence keeping you further from death.

Level 1

You gain an additional Minor Consequence Slot on your Consequence track:

0 1 1 2 3 4 0
Healthy Minor Minor Mod' Major Crit' Terminal
Level 2

As level 1 plus you acquire another Moderate Consequence Slot on your Consequence track:

0 1 1 2 2 3 4 0
Healthy Minor Minor Mod' Mod' Major Crit' Terminal
Level 3

As level 2 plus you get yet another Mu Minor Consequence Slots on your Consequence track:

0 1 1 1 2 2 3 4 0
Healthy Minor Minor Minor Mod' Mod' Major Crit' Terminal

Never Say Die Talents

Cast Iron (1 skill Point)

You recover quicker than most. Through rest and natural healing, you can remove a Minor (Physical) Consequence after 8 minutes and a Moderate (Physical) Consequence after 3 hours.

Thick Blood (1 skill Point)

Major (Physical) Consequences and Critical (Physical) Consequences you have suffered take an additional 15 minutes each to worsen.

Stubborn (1 skill point)

You recover quicker than most. Through rest and natural healing, you can remove a Minor (Psyche) Consequence after 8 minutes and a Moderate (Psyche) Consequence after 3 hours.

Self Esteem (1 skill point)

Major (Psyche) Consequences and Critical (Psyche) Consequences you have suffered take an additional 15 minutes each to worsen.

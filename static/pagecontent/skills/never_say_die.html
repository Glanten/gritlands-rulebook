Never Say Die Talents

Cast Iron (1 skill Point)

You recover Minor and Moderate (physical) consequences quicker than others: after 8 minutes and 3 hours respectively.

Thick Blood (1 skill Point)

Your Moderate and Critical (physical) consequences take an additional 15 minutes each to worsen.

Stubborn (1 skill point)

You recover Minor and Moderate (psyche) consequences faster than others: after 8 minutes and 3 hours respectively.

Self Esteem (1 skill point)

Your Moderate and Critical (psyche) consequences take an additional 15 minutes each to worsen.

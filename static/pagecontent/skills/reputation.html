Reputation

Everypony

This skill represents your (off-screen) standing within the community; the political and social pressure you can bring to bear, and your popularity back home.

This skill may impact how many individuals you can affect with your clan's tradition and the degree to which certain skills affect you and others.

Reputation also weights your votes when deciding what to do with esoteric artefacts from the beforetimes, when choosing your clan's long-term plans, and when divying up the spoils of a successful HOTZONE delve.

Reputation is a utility skill that makes you a more influential decision-maker between HOTZONES. It also increases the limits on clan traditions, allowing more people to restore more GRIT from a single instance of a tradition.

Level 1
  • You will receive more resources from the central pool than any character who has no Reputation.
  • You have an extra vote towards communal decisions in The Home Front.
Level 2
  • As level 1 plus:
  • You will receive more resources from the central pool than any character who has only Reputation 1.
  • You have another extra vote for The Home Front.
Level 3
  • As level 2 plus:
  • Even characters with Reputation 2 will be jealous of your favourable draw from the central pool.
  • You yet another (yes, that's right!) extra vote for use in The Home Front.

Reputation Talents

Voting Block (1 skill point)

If your group has 5 members you gain an extra vote for The Home Front.

It's Who you Know (1 skill point)

Requires Reputation 3.

You have an additional vote.

Tribute (1 skill point)

You receive a number of random resources equal to your Reputation at the start of each event.

Network (1 skill point)

You receive information, at the beginning of each event, about the state of the HOTZONE, the surrounding territory, and other interesting subjects that may be of assistance. The higher your Reputation the more specific and useful the information you receive.

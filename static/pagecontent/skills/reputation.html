Reputation

Everypony

This skill represents your (off screen) standing within the community, the political and social pressure you can bring to bear, and your popularity back home.

This skill impacts how many individuals you can affect with some Clan based skills and the degree to which certain skills affect you and others.

You are also able to leverage your Reputation to get access to equipment, and a bigger personal share of the communal resources.

Level 1
  • You will receive more resources from the central pool than any player who has no Reputation.
  • You have 1 extra vote towards communal decisions (next Hot Zone, Base Upgrades, Policy decisions).
Level 2
  • You will receive more resources from the central pool than any player who has 1 Reputation.
  • You have 2 extra votes towards communal decisions (next Hot Zone, Base Upgrades, Policy decisions).
Level 3
  • You will receive more resources from the central pool than any player who has 2 Reputation.
  • You have 3 extra votes towards communal decisions (next Hotzone, Base Upgrades, Policy decisions).

Reputation Talents

Voting Block (1 skill point)

If your group has 5 members you gain 1 additional vote.

It's who you know (1 skill point)

Requires Reputation level 3

You have 1 additional vote.

Tribute (1 skill point)

You receive a number of random resources equal to your Reputation level at the start of each event.

Network (1 skill point)

You gain an amount of random information about the state of a Hot Zone and the surrounding territory based on your Reputation level at the start of each event.

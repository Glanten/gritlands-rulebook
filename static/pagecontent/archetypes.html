Archetypes

There are 5 broad archetypes available. Archetypes provide a mechanical benefit by grouping together related skills and making them cheaper to purchase and advance. Archetypes also fit within their clan in a clearly defined way. Rather than picking an Archetype, you can choose to be an Oddball. Weirdo.

Scout

Primarily concerned with assessing and surveying the blasted wasteland, Scouts tend to be tough, stealthy, survivors, able to assess the lay of the land, spot and circumvent the many hazards and dangers that the wastes, and the Hot Zones throw at them. They are INVALUABLE - without them you will get poisoned, irradiated, ambushed or worse.

Warrior

Everyone knows to carry a big stick and conserve ammo for the worst of circumstances. But warriors excel at taking and dealing out raw violence. A handy trick in such a brutal, violent world inhabited by monsters, horrors and unscrupulous or psychotic survivors who will murder you dead as soon as look at you. They are INVALUABLE - without them you stand no chance against the hordes of beings who want to kill you and wear your skin like a dress.

Scientist

You need every edge you can get in Hot Zones and Drugs will often be the first, last and most fun way to get that edge. But Even then - sometimes - you will get shot, stabbed, beaten and blown up. When this happens you are going to need someone to patch you up and make the pain go away. They are INVALUABLE - as a shot of Adreno-Bang might be the only thing to get you through, and no one wants to try sewing their own intestines back into their body.

Engineer

If drugs give you the edge then the rest of the shape is made up of your gear. Without it you might as well not leave the safety of the enclaves. Not that there would be enclaves without engineers - they keep everything running in good order and use their ingenuity and opposable thumbs to solve problems that would otherwise be beyond the abilities of anyone. They are INVALUABLE - as you are worse than toast without decent gear.

Enablers

The strength of the enclaves - and of humanity itself - its very survival through the horrors of the last centuries are entirely dependent on humankind's ability to cooperate (or at the very least coordinate). Enablers hold society together, and drive it to become greater than the sum of its parts. They are INVALUABLE - because without them you are nothing more than a solitary lost monkey screaming into the face of annihilation.

Oddballs

Some people are just square pegs. But sometimes that is what you need.
